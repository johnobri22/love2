Introduction

We are all actors, some stage like some screen like. If you’re late for work and the barista at Starbucks is slow making your coffee, the introverts (“the screen like”) among us would call various face muscles into action to communicate the rush we’re in. The extroverts among us often adapt “stage like” acting to fulfill their desires. For example if your child is misbehaving at Disneyland what better way to correct them than to evoke the attention and support of nearby holidaymakers by roaring “Bums are for seats, not the ground!"
